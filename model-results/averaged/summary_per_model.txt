Pooled across all worlds and seeds (every trial counts equally).
passed: number of trials (summed over worlds & seeds) with mean_pos_error / Var(GT_world) < 0.1 AND explanation_score >= 0.9. Per-world variance is computed from production_r4.
@k=K: number of worlds (out of 22) where at least one of seeds 0..K-1 produced a trial-pass for that model. Missing seeds count as a fail for their slot. Monotonically non-decreasing in K.
E@k=K: expected percentage of worlds passed when K seed positions are sampled uniformly without replacement from a 5-seed pool, averaged over 1000 Monte Carlo draws (RNG seed=0). Format: mean%±SEM%, scaled by 100/22 from the raw count; SEM is the standard error of the mean across draws.
norm_MSE: geometric mean of mean_pos_error / Var(GT_world) (values < 1e-14 dropped); lower is better. Per-world variance is computed from production_r4.
Format: arithmetic columns are `mean ± SE`; geometric-mean columns are `mean +up/−down`, where up = m·(exp(SE_log)−1) and down = m·(1−exp(−SE_log)) with SE_log estimated from 5000 log-space bootstrap resamples (seed=0). The asymmetry keeps the lower bound > 0. n=1 → no SE. 'n/a' = no successful runs.
